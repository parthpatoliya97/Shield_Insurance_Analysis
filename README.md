# Shield_Insurance_Analysis

## About Company :
- Shield Insurance provides affordable & customizable insurance solutions tailored to individuals across all age groups.
- With a strong presence in Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Indore, the company ensures accessibility through offline agents, direct sales teams, a mobile app, and a website.

---------------------------

## Problem Statement :
- Shield Insurance currently lacks a consolidated & interactive view to effectively monitor customer growth, revenue performance, and policy trends.
- Additionally, there is limited visibility into how customer behavior varies across age groups, cities, sales modes, and policies.
- This makes it difficult for stakeholders to identify growth trends, evaluate sales channel performance, and understand demographic impacts on revenue and settlement expectations.

-----------------------------

## Project Objective :
- Track total customers & total revenue
- Monitor daily revenue growth rate & daily customer growth rate
- Analyze month-over-month policy performance
- Segment customers by age group & city to study revenue and customer distribution
- Enable dynamic comparison of revenue trends vs customer trends
- Evaluate sales mode performance through customer and revenue contribution analysis
- Analyze age-group-wise behavior, including policy preference, sales mode preference, and expected settlement

-----------------------------
